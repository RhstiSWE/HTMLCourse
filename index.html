Mahber Theater Asmara

Who are Matea?

Mahber tyatr Asmara (Mat-A) or Asmara Theater Association was found by interested citizens to push for an Eritrean culture and national renaissance. Besides the association's goals of promoting Eritrean culture and identity, it also strongly pushed to empower and embolden the youth to stand up against Ethiopain rule by promoting resistance through music and poetry. For this reason, the association was always under heavy surveillance and censorship by the Ethiopian government. but this didn't stop many of the members from writing many cryptic songs instilling patriotic fervor in the youth
